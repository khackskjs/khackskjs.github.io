찾다 - Find, Look for, Search

Youtube의 영어회화 | 어휘 | ‘찾다’의 3가지 모습 - find | look for | search를 기억하기 위해 정리한 내용입니다.

Sumary

한문장에 끝내기:
I’ve been looking for them for 2 hours. I can’t find them. I searched Every where

의미 example
Find 찾는 순간(일시적) I found my keys.
Look for 과정 I’m looking for my note.
Search (지역/공간) 뒤지다 I searched everywhere

| Usage | I’ll ask her when I see her | I’m going to give a presentation in class |

Find

발견하는 그 순간

  • I found my keys.
  • I found my cellphone.

Look for

찾는 과정

  • I’m looking for my keys.
  • I’m looking for a good restaurant in this area.
  • I’m looking for a something to do this weekend.
  • Are you looking for something?
  • what are you looking for?

Search

(공간,장소 등을) 뒤지다/수색하다.

  • I searched everywhere and I still can’t find my keys.
  • A. Did you search your room?
  • B. Yeah, I searched my room, I searched my pockets, I searched my coat pockets.
  • I’ve been looking for them for 2 hours. I can’t find them. I searched Every where
  • I searched the internet and I found some information about the brand.

Search for

  • I searched for some information online
  • I searched for some information on the internet
  • I serached to find some information
